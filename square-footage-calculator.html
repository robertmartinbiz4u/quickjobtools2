How to Calculate Square Footage

To find square footage, multiply the length of the area by the width of the area. Both measurements should be in feet.

Formula: length × width = square feet

Example: 12 feet × 10 feet = 120 square feet.

Square Footage FAQ

How do I calculate square footage?

Multiply length by width. For example, a 12-foot by 10-foot room is 120 square feet.

What measurements should I enter?

Enter both length and width in feet. If you have inches, convert them to feet first or use the feet to inches converter.

Can this be used for concrete slabs?

Yes. This gives the area of the slab in square feet. For concrete volume, use the concrete bag calculator.

Can I use decimals?

Yes. Decimal values like 12.5 feet or 8.75 feet work.
