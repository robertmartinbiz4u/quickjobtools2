How to Calculate Gravel

Multiply area by depth to estimate volume. Convert depth from inches to feet by dividing by 12.

Formula: area × depth in feet = cubic feet

Divide cubic feet by 27 to convert to cubic yards.

Helpful Tip

Add 5% to 10% extra gravel for compaction, uneven ground, spreading differences, and waste.

Gravel Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate gravel needed?

Multiply area in square feet by depth in feet to get cubic feet. Divide by 27 to get cubic yards.

What gravel depth should I use?

Common depths range from 2 to 4 inches for landscaping and can be deeper for driveways or base layers.

Should I add extra gravel?

Yes. Adding 5 to 10 percent extra is useful for compaction, waste, and uneven areas.
