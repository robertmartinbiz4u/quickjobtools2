How to Estimate Drywall Sheets

Divide total wall area by the square footage of one drywall sheet, then round up.

Formula: wall area ÷ sheet area = sheets needed

Drywall Sheet Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate drywall sheets?

Divide total wall area by the square footage of one sheet, then round up.

Should I add extra drywall?

Yes. Add 10 to 15 percent extra for cuts, waste, and mistakes.

Does this subtract windows and doors?

No. Subtract non-drywalled areas first for a more accurate estimate.
