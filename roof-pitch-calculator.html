How to Calculate Roof Pitch

Roof pitch is commonly shown as rise over 12 inches of run. A 6-inch rise over 12 inches of run is a 6/12 pitch.

Formula: pitch = rise ÷ run

Roof Pitch Calculator FAQ

What is roof pitch?

Roof pitch is the rise of the roof compared to its horizontal run, commonly shown as inches of rise per 12 inches of run.

How do I calculate roof pitch angle?

Divide rise by run, then use arctangent to calculate the angle.

What does 6/12 pitch mean?

A 6/12 pitch means the roof rises 6 inches for every 12 inches of horizontal run.
