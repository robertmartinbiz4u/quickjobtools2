How to Calculate Weekly Pay

To calculate weekly gross pay, multiply your hourly rate by regular hours worked, then add overtime pay if applicable.

Formula: regular pay + overtime pay = weekly gross pay

Example: $25/hour × 40 regular hours = $1,000. If you also work 5 overtime hours at 1.5×, overtime pay is $187.50, for total weekly gross pay of $1,187.50.
