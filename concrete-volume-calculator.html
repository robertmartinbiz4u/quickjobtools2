How to Calculate Concrete Volume

Multiply length by width by thickness. Convert thickness from inches to feet by dividing by 12.

Formula: length × width × thickness in feet = cubic feet

How do I calculate concrete volume?

There are 27 cubic feet in one cubic yard.
