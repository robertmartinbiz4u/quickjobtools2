About

Quick Job Tools is a collection of simple calculators and converters designed for construction, labor, landscaping, and trade work.

What this site is

These tools are built to be fast, mobile-friendly, and easy to use on the job. No accounts, no sign-ups, and no unnecessary steps.

How to use the tools

Enter your measurements or values and review the result. All outputs are estimates for planning purposes. Always confirm measurements, materials, and requirements for your specific job.
