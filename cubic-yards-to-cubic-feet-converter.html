How to Convert Cubic Yards to Cubic Feet

One cubic yard equals 27 cubic feet. To convert cubic yards to cubic feet, multiply the number of cubic yards by 27.

Formula: cubic yards × 27 = cubic feet

Example: 2 cubic yards × 27 = 54 cubic feet.
