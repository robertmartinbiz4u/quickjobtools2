How to Calculate Mulch Volume

To calculate mulch volume, multiply the area by the mulch depth. Since depth is usually measured in inches, convert inches to feet first by dividing by 12.

Formula: area × depth in feet = cubic feet

To convert cubic feet to cubic yards, divide by 27.

Mulch Volume FAQ

How do I calculate mulch needed?

Multiply the area in square feet by the mulch depth in feet to get cubic feet. Divide by 27 to get cubic yards.

What depth should I use for mulch?

Most mulch projects use 2 to 4 inches of depth. A 3-inch depth is a common general estimate.

Should I add extra mulch?

Yes. Adding 5% to 10% extra helps account for uneven ground, waste, and settling.

How many cubic feet are in a cubic yard?

There are 27 cubic feet in one cubic yard.
