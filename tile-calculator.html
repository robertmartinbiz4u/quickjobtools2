How to Calculate Tile Quantity

Divide the project area by the area of one tile, then add waste and round up.

Formula: area ÷ tile area = tiles needed

Helpful Tip

Add 10% to 15% extra tile for cuts, breakage, pattern matching, and future repairs.

Tile Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate how many tiles I need?

Divide the total area by the square footage of one tile, add waste, and round up.

How much extra tile should I buy?

Many projects add 10 to 15 percent extra for cuts, waste, breakage, and repairs.

Can I use this for floors and walls?

Yes. This calculator can be used for floor tile, wall tile, backsplashes, and similar areas.
