How to Convert Feet to Inches

To convert feet to inches, multiply the number of feet by 12.

Formula: feet × 12 = inches

Example: 6 feet × 12 = 72 inches.

Feet to Inches FAQ

How many inches are in a foot?

There are 12 inches in one foot.

How do you convert feet to inches?

Multiply the number of feet by 12. For example, 7 feet equals 84 inches.

Can I convert inches back to feet?

Yes. Enter a value in the inches box and the calculator will automatically convert it back to feet.

Can I use decimal feet?

Yes. For example, 6.5 feet equals 78 inches.
